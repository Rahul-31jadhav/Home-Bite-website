Privacy Policy & guidelines

A privacy policy is a legal document that outlines how an organization collects, uses, discloses, and manages a user’s or client’s personal data. It’s a crucial part of data compliance, transparency and building user trust, especially with the increasing number of data privacy laws.

Privacy Policy

At HomeBite, we value your trust. This Privacy Policy & Guidelines outline how we collect, use, and protect your information, and how we ensure a safe, respectful, and enjoyable dining experience for both guests and hosts.

By using our app or website, you agree to the terms described below. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please discontinue use of our website.

1. Information we collect

We may collect personal and non-personal information, including:

  • Personal Data: Name, email, phone number, address, and payment details.
  • Usage Data: Device type, IP address, location for delivery/pick-up, browsing patterns, Meal Preferences, Allergies, dietary restrictions, and favorite cuisines (if provided).
  • Transaction Data: Order history, cancellations, and feedback ratings.

2. How We User Your Information

We use the information to:

  • Facilitate secure bookings and payments.
  • Connect guests with verified hosts.
  • Send important updates, and notifications.
  • Ensure food safety and community trust.
  • Provide customer support and resolve disputes.

3. Sharing of Information

We only share information with:

  • Hosts (necessary details for meal preparation & delivery).
  • Third-party providers (payment gateways, analytics, or delivery partners).
  • Send important updates, and notifications.
  • Legal authorities if required by law.

4. Your Privacy Choices

You may choose to:

  • Update or correct your profile information anytime.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.
  • Request account deletion and removal of personal data.
  • Control cookies and app permissions.

5. Data Security

We implement strict security protocols to safeguard your data, including:

  • Encrypted transactions and secure servers.
  • Access restricted to authorized personnel only.
  • Regular security audits and system monitoring.

7. Host Guidelines

  • Maintain hygiene and prepare meals safely.
  • Provide accurate meal details, photos, and prices.
  • Respect guest allergies and dietary preferences.

9. Updates to this Policy

  • We may update these policies from time to time. All updates will be posted here with the revised “Effective Date.”

10. Purposes for the collection of personal Information

We collect personal information to operate, manage and maintain our business to provide our services adn to accomplish our business purposes and objectives, including to:

  • Ensure smooth browsing, ordering, and meal booking experiences.
  • Provide customer support and respond to meal-related inquiries or complaints.
  • Maintain and manage guest and host accounts or registrations.
  • Personalize recommendations and improve user experiences on the platform.
  • Conduct research, analytics, and reporting to enhance our services.
  • Operate, maintain, and secure our digital platform and infrastructure.
  • Carry out quality checks and ensure food safety compliance.

  • Facilitate payments, refunds, and any financial reconciliations.
